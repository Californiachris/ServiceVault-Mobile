WORKFLOWS TO BUILD:
1. Worker Check-In:
   • Worker scans QR/NFC tag at property
   • System verifies GPS or NFC proximity (within 50–100 ft)
   • Worker selects their role (maintenance, cleaner, landscaper, inspector, etc.)
   • Assigned tasks instantly load based on property manager’s list

2. Task Completion Flow:
   • Worker checks off tasks one by one
   • Optional photo/video upload per task
   • Notes field for issues or recommendations

3. Worker Check-Out:
   • Worker presses “Complete & Scan Out”
   • QR/NFC verification again + timestamp
   • Full visit summary logged in property record
   • Instant notification sent to manager & owner
